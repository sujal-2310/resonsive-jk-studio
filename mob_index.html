ABOUT US

Photos are the token of our life events. They remain in the memory, and after so many years, when we see them again, they actually help us to feel the same way. There are many mega events of our life in which we hire professional photographers. At present time, marriage photo shoots are in huge demand, and actually, the skilled and experienced photographers are not always available as per the expectations of the people.

When photography was not adored by many, there were two passionate professional photographers in India working tirelessly and wholeheartedly to make the memories of their clients so awesome.

In the year 1995, Mr. Mahesh Prajapati started his brand known as JK Photo Studio at Mehesana, Gujarat. His son Mr. Parth Prajapati also joined him, and from then, they have been setting high standards in the field of photography and cinematography. Having the experience of more than 29 years, they have made a special space in the hearts and memories of more than 300+ clients.

JK Photo Studio provides services as follows:

  • Engagement Ceremony
  • Pre-Wedding Photography
  • Candid Photography
  • Wedding Events
  • Birthday Party
  • Baby Shower

Memories are hard to remove and effortful to save! But with us (JK Photo Studio), that’s not a big deal anymore! Our unique photography skills would save your time, money, and precious memories that’ll be alive in the next many generations! Contact us now!
